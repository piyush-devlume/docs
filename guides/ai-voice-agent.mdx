Vobiz provides the telephony layer—real phone numbers, SIP trunking, and call routing—so you can wire any conversational AI platform directly to the public phone network without managing WebRTC streams yourself. This guide explains how the architecture works and points you to the integration guide for each supported platform.

## How it works

An AI voice agent built on Vobiz consists of three layers working together:

1. **Telecom layer (Vobiz):** Owns the phone number (DID), terminates inbound calls, originates outbound calls, and manages the SIP session lifecycle.
2. **Interconnect:** Vobiz forwards audio to your AI platform via a SIP URI or a bidirectional WebSocket media stream (`<Connect><Stream>`).
3. **AI layer:** The AI provider runs speech-to-text (STT), sends context to an LLM, generates a response, and converts it back to audio with text-to-speech (TTS) before streaming it to the caller.

<Info>
You do not need to write any low-level audio code for SIP-based integrations (VAPI, Retell AI, ElevenLabs, LiveKit). Vobiz handles all RTP media handling. WebSocket-based integrations (Pipecat, raw WebSocket) give you byte-level control when you need it.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

Before setting up your first agent, make sure you have:

- An active [Vobiz account](https://console.vobiz.ai/auth/signup) with account balance loaded
- At least one phone number purchased in the Vobiz Console
- An account with your chosen AI voice platform (see below)

## Setup overview

Every integration follows the same four steps regardless of platform:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a SIP trunk in Vobiz">
    Log in to the [Vobiz Console](https://console.vobiz.ai) and navigate to **SIP Trunk → Outbound Trunks → Trunks**. Create a trunk and copy the **SIP domain**, **username**, and **password** from the Authentication & Linking section. You will paste these values into your AI platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your AI platform">
    Follow the platform-specific guide linked above. You will enter your Vobiz SIP domain and credentials so the AI platform can route calls through Vobiz.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your phone number">
    In the Vobiz Console, purchase a phone number and associate it with your SIP trunk. For inbound calling, set the trunk's inbound destination to the SIP URI provided by your AI platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test the call">
    Place or receive a test call. Verify that audio flows end-to-end and check call records in both the Vobiz Console and your AI platform dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>
