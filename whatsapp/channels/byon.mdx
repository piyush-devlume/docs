The BYON (Bring Your Own Number) method lets you connect an existing phone number to WhatsApp Business through Vobiz — with no additional number cost. This guide covers the full process from Meta Business Manager setup to completing the connection in Vobiz.

<Info>
  **Time required:** 15–20 minutes if you have Meta Business Manager access. Make sure your number is **not** currently registered with WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app before starting.
</Info>

## Prerequisites

- Meta Business Manager account ([business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com))
- A phone number that can receive SMS or voice calls for OTP verification
- Admin or Developer role in Meta Business Manager
- Active Vobiz account ([console.vobiz.ai](https://console.vobiz.ai))

## Setup steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up Meta Business Manager">
    Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com) and log in. Create a business account if you don't have one, or select your existing business.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)">
    1. Click the menu icon and navigate to **Accounts → WhatsApp Accounts**.
    2. Click **Add** and fill in:
       - **WhatsApp Business Account Name** — your business name
       - **Business Manager** — select your business
       - **Time Zone** — your business timezone
    3. After creation, copy your **WABA ID** (a 15–17 digit number, e.g. `102290129340398`). You'll need this for Vobiz.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add and verify your phone number">
    1. In your WhatsApp Business Account, click **Add phone number**.
    2. Enter your number in international format (e.g., `+1 234 567 8900`).
    3. Choose SMS or voice call for verification and enter the 6-digit code.
    4. Once verified, click the phone number to view details and copy the **Phone Number ID** (e.g., `106540932419283`).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a System User and access token">
    1. Go to **Business Settings → Users → System Users** and click **Add**.
    2. Name it (e.g., `Vobiz Integration`), set role to **Admin**, and click **Create System User**.
    3. Click **Add Assets**, select **WhatsApp Accounts**, choose your WABA, enable **Full control**, and save.
    4. Click **Generate New Token**, select your app, and enable these permissions:
       - `whatsapp_business_messaging`
       - `whatsapp_business_management`
    5. Copy the token immediately and store it securely — you won't be able to view it again.

    <Warning>
      The access token grants full access to your WhatsApp Business Account. Never expose it in client-side code or version control.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect to Vobiz">
    1. Log in to [console.vobiz.ai](https://console.vobiz.ai).
    2. Go to **Messaging → Channels** and click **Connect Channel → Bring Your Own Number (BYON)**.
    3. Fill in the form with the values you collected:

    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | WABA ID | From Step 2 (e.g., `102290129340398`) |
    | Phone Number ID | From Step 3 (e.g., `106540932419283`) |
    | Access Token | System user token from Step 4 (starts with `EAA`) |
    | Display Name | How your business name appears to customers |

    4. Click **Connect Channel**. Vobiz verifies your credentials and the channel status changes to **Connected**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invalid Access Token">
    - Token may have expired or been regenerated
    - Token missing required permissions (`whatsapp_business_messaging`, `whatsapp_business_management`)
    - System user doesn't have access to the WABA

    **Fix:** Generate a new token with correct permissions and verify system user has Full control on the WABA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Phone Number Not Found">
    - Incorrect Phone Number ID
    - Number not verified in Meta (no green checkmark)
    - Number not associated with the WABA

    **Fix:** Double-check the Phone Number ID in Meta Business Manager and confirm the number is verified under the correct WABA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="WABA Not Accessible">
    - Incorrect WABA ID
    - WABA is suspended or restricted
    - System user lacks access

    **Fix:** Verify the WABA ID under Meta Business Settings and ensure the system user is assigned to the WABA.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can't generate an access token">
    - No Meta app created yet
    - Insufficient Business Manager permissions

    **Fix:** Create a Meta app at [developers.facebook.com](https://developers.facebook.com), add the WhatsApp product, and ensure you have Admin role in Business Manager.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
