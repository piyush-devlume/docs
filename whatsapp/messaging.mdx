Vobiz gives you a full suite of tools for sending and receiving WhatsApp messages — from a point-and-click inbox to a complete REST API. This page covers message types, the 24-hour messaging window, how to use the inbox, template messages, and broadcast campaigns.

## Message types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Text">
    Send plain text messages up to 4,096 characters. Text messages support markdown-style formatting, URLs with link previews, emoji, and line breaks.

    ```json
    {
      "type": "text",
      "text": {
        "body": "Hi! Your appointment is confirmed for tomorrow at 10 AM.",
        "preview_url": false
      }
    }
    ```
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Media">
    Share images, videos, documents, and audio files, each with an optional caption.

    | Format | Types | Max size |
    | --- | --- | --- |
    | Images | JPEG, PNG | 5 MB |
    | Videos | MP4, 3GP | 16 MB |
    | Audio | AAC, MP3, OGG | 16 MB |
    | Documents | PDF, DOC, XLS, and more | 100 MB |

    Media files must be hosted at a publicly accessible HTTPS URL.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Template">
    Pre-approved message formats required for business-initiated conversations outside the 24-hour window. Templates support variable placeholders, headers, footers, and buttons.

    See the [Template messages](#template-messages) section below for details on creating and sending templates.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Interactive">
    Messages with embedded actions that customers can tap to respond:

    - **Reply buttons** — up to 3 quick-reply options
    - **List messages** — a scrollable menu of options
    - **Call-to-action buttons** — open a URL or dial a phone number

    Interactive messages are available within the 24-hour messaging window.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## The 24-hour messaging window

WhatsApp enforces a 24-hour window to protect users from unsolicited outbound messages.

### Within 24 hours

When a customer sends you a message, a 24-hour window opens. During this window you can send:

- Free-form text messages
- Media and documents
- Interactive messages
- Multiple messages in a row

No templates required. Each message the customer sends resets the 24-hour timer.

### After 24 hours

Once 24 hours pass without a customer message, you can only send **pre-approved template messages**. Sending a template re-opens a 24-hour window.

<Warning>
  Never attempt to send free-form messages outside the 24-hour window. The request will fail with an error from Meta's API. Use a template to restart the conversation.
</Warning>

## Using the inbox

The Vobiz inbox gives you a unified interface to manage all WhatsApp conversations across your connected channels.

### Interface layout

The inbox is divided into four sections:

1. **Channel selector (top)** — Switch between WhatsApp numbers if you have multiple channels.
2. **Conversation list (left)** — Shows all conversations with contact name, last message preview, timestamp, unread badge, and a 24-hour window countdown.
3. **Message thread (center)** — Full message history with delivery status indicators, media previews, and a message composer at the bottom.
4. **Contact panel (right)** — Contact details, tags, custom attributes, conversation notes, and activity timeline.

### Sending messages from the inbox

**Text messages:** Select a conversation (or click **New Message**), type in the composer, and press **Enter** to send. Use **Shift+Enter** for a line break.

**Media messages:** Click the attachment icon in the composer, choose the file type, select a file, add an optional caption, and send.

**Template messages:** Click the template icon in the composer, pick a template from your library, fill in any variable placeholders, preview the message, and send. Use templates whenever the 24-hour window has expired.

**Canned responses:** Type `/` in the composer to browse saved replies. Click any canned response to insert it, edit as needed, then send.

### Message status indicators

| Status | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Queued | Message is waiting to be sent |
| Sent | Accepted by WhatsApp's servers |
| Delivered | Received on the customer's device |
| Read | Customer opened the message |
| Failed | Delivery failed — check the error details |

### Conversation management

Use the search bar and filters to find conversations quickly:

- **Search** by contact name, phone number, or message content
- **Filter by status:** All, Unread, Active (within 24h window), Expired
- **Filter by channel:** View conversations from a specific WhatsApp number
- **Filter by tags:** Show conversations with a specific label

From any conversation you can mark as read/unread, add or remove tags, leave internal notes, assign to a team member, archive, or delete.

## Template messages

Templates are pre-approved message formats that Meta requires for all business-initiated conversations outside the 24-hour window. Every template must pass Meta's review before you can send it.

### Template categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Utility">
    Transactional messages about ongoing business transactions or customer accounts.

    **Examples:** order status updates, delivery notifications, payment confirmations, account changes.

    Utility templates go through standard approval and have the least restrictive content requirements.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Marketing">
    Promotional messages including offers, announcements, and product recommendations.

    **Examples:** new product launches, seasonal promotions, event invitations, flash sales.

    <Note>
      Marketing templates have stricter content requirements. Always include clear opt-out instructions in the footer (e.g., "Reply STOP to unsubscribe").
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Authentication">
    One-time passcodes for user verification and two-factor authentication.

    **Examples:** login codes, password reset codes, transaction verification.

    Authentication templates have expedited approval and support a dedicated **Copy Code** button for one-tap OTP copying.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Creating a template

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Templates">
    Go to **Messaging → Templates** and click **Create Template**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set basic information">
    Enter a template name (lowercase, underscores instead of spaces), choose a category (Utility, Marketing, or Authentication), and select a language from the 60+ supported options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build the template content">
    A template can include up to four components:

    - **Header (optional):** Text (60 chars max), image, video, or document
    - **Body (required):** Main message text up to 1,024 characters. Use `{{1}}`, `{{2}}` for variable placeholders and `*bold*` / `_italic_` for formatting.
    - **Footer (optional):** Small text up to 60 characters — often used for opt-out instructions
    - **Buttons (optional):** Up to 3 buttons — Quick Reply, URL (can include variables), Phone Number, or Copy Code (Authentication only)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for approval">
    Review the preview and click **Submit**. Meta typically approves or rejects templates within a few hours to 24 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Creating a template via API

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.vobiz.ai/v1/messaging/templates \
  -H "X-Auth-ID: YOUR_AUTH_ID" \
  -H "X-Auth-Token: YOUR_AUTH_TOKEN" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "name": "order_confirmation",
    "category": "UTILITY",
    "language": "en",
    "components": [
      {
        "type": "HEADER",
        "format": "TEXT",
        "text": "Order confirmed"
      },
      {
        "type": "BODY",
        "text": "Hi {{1}}, your order #{{2}} has been confirmed and will arrive by {{3}}."
      },
      {
        "type": "FOOTER",
        "text": "Thank you for shopping with us!"
      },
      {
        "type": "BUTTONS",
        "buttons": [
          {
            "type": "URL",
            "text": "Track order",
            "url": "https://example.com/track/{{1}}"
          }
        ]
      }
    ]
  }'
```

### Sending a template via API

```bash
curl -X POST https://api.vobiz.ai/v1/messaging/send \
  -H "X-Auth-ID: YOUR_AUTH_ID" \
  -H "X-Auth-Token: YOUR_AUTH_TOKEN" \
  -H "Content-Type: application/json" \
  -d '{
    "channel_id": "ch_abc123",
    "to": "+1234567890",
    "type": "template",
    "template": {
      "name": "order_confirmation",
      "language": "en",
      "parameters": [
        { "type": "text", "text": "Jane Smith" },
        { "type": "text", "text": "ORD-98765" },
        { "type": "text", "text": "April 30, 2026" }
      ]
    }
  }'
```

### Common rejection reasons

- Spelling or grammar errors
- Unclear message purpose
- Misleading or false content
- Variables that don't make sense in context
- Marketing content submitted as Utility category
- Policy violations

If Meta rejects your template, correct the issue and resubmit. You can resubmit templates as many times as needed.

## Broadcast campaigns

Broadcast campaigns let you send a template message to many contacts at once. Campaigns are ideal for announcements, promotions, or bulk transactional notifications.

**To create a campaign:**

1. Go to **Messaging → Campaigns** and click **Create Campaign**.
2. Choose a channel and select an approved template.
3. Define your audience — select contacts by tag, import a CSV, or choose from your full contact list.
4. Set a send time: immediately or at a scheduled date and time.
5. Review the preview, confirm recipient count, and launch.

The campaign dashboard shows delivery, read, and response statistics in real time as messages go out.

<Warning>
  Only send campaigns to contacts who have opted in to receive messages from your business. Sending unsolicited promotional messages violates WhatsApp's Business Policy and can result in your number being suspended.
</Warning>

## Messaging best practices

- **Respond quickly.** Aim to reply within five minutes during business hours. Fast response times improve customer satisfaction and keep your Meta quality rating high.
- **Personalize messages.** Use customer names and reference prior interactions. Personalized messages reduce block and report rates.
- **Keep messages short.** Messages under 500 characters are easier to read on mobile and get better response rates.
- **Use media purposefully.** Images and video increase engagement but use data. Only send media when it adds real value.
- **Avoid over-messaging.** Multiple messages when one would do can lead to blocks and a lower quality rating.
- **Respect opt-outs immediately.** Stop messaging any contact who requests it and maintain an opt-out list.
