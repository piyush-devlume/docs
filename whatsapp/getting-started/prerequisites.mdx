Before you can send or receive WhatsApp messages through Vobiz, you need a few things set up on the Meta side. This page walks through each requirement so you're ready for the quick-start guide.

<Info>
  Setup typically takes **15–30 minutes** if you're starting from scratch. If you already have a Meta Business Account, it's much faster.
</Info>

## 1. Meta Business Account

A Meta Business Account gives you access to WhatsApp Business API (and other Meta business tools). It's free to create.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to business.facebook.com">
    Log in with your personal Facebook account. You'll need this to manage a Business Account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your business">
    Click **Create Account** in the top right and enter your business name and details. Use your official business name — Meta requires it for verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete verification">
    For production use and higher message limits, Meta requires business verification (typically 1–3 business days). You can start testing while awaiting approval.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## 2. WhatsApp Business Account (WABA)

Within Meta Business Manager, create a WhatsApp Business Account (WABA):

1. Go to **Accounts → WhatsApp Accounts** in Meta Business Manager.
2. Click **Add → Create a WhatsApp Business Account**.
3. Follow the wizard to create the WABA.
4. **Save your WABA ID** — find it under WhatsApp Manager → Settings → Business Info (format: `123456789012345`).

## 3. Phone number

Your phone number must meet these requirements:

- Can receive voice calls or SMS (used for OTP verification)
- **Not** currently registered with WhatsApp or WhatsApp Business app — deregister it first if needed
- In a country supported by Meta's WhatsApp Business API

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Bring Your Own Number (BYON)">
    Use an existing number you own. Free, but requires OTP verification and takes 5–10 minutes. See the [BYON setup guide](/whatsapp/channels/byon) for full instructions.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Buy from Vobiz">
    Purchase a WhatsApp-capable number directly from Vobiz. Instant setup, no verification needed, but a monthly fee applies. Choose from available countries in the console.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## 4. Vobiz account

You need an active Vobiz account with:

- **Auth ID and Auth Token** — available in the Vobiz console under **Settings → API Credentials** (needed for API calls)
- **Sufficient balance** — WhatsApp conversations are billed per session; add credits before going live

Sign up at [console.vobiz.ai](https://console.vobiz.ai) if you don't have an account.

## Ready?

Once these are in place, follow the [Quick Start guide](/whatsapp/getting-started/quick-start) to connect your first channel and send a message.
