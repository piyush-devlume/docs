## Get started in four steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Register at [console.vobiz.ai](https://console.vobiz.ai/app/register). You'll receive ₹25 in free credit to explore the platform.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get a phone number">
    Purchase a DID phone number from the console or via the [Phone Numbers API](/account-phone-number/purchase-from-inventory). Filter by region to find the right number.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up a SIP trunk or application">
    Create a [SIP trunk](/trunks/overview) to connect your own infrastructure, or set up an [XML application](/xml/overview) to control call flows with webhooks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make and receive calls">
    Use the [Calls API](/call/make-call), your preferred [SDK](/integrations/node-sdk), or a connected [AI platform](/guides/ai-voice-agent) to start handling calls.
  </Step>
</Steps>
