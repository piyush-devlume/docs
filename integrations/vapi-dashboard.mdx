This guide shows you how to connect your Vobiz SIP trunk to VAPI so your AI assistant can make and receive real phone calls. The entire setup is done through the VAPI dashboard—no code or curl commands required. Expect to finish in about 15 minutes.

**Outbound call flow:**

```text
Your App → VAPI API → Vobiz SIP → Phone Network → Person Answers → AI Assistant Speaks
```

## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A Vobiz account with an active SIP trunk — [sign up](https://console.vobiz.ai/auth/signup)
- A VAPI account — [sign up](https://dashboard.vapi.ai/signup)
- A VAPI assistant already created — [create one](https://dashboard.vapi.ai)
- A phone number purchased from Vobiz (for caller ID)

## Outbound calling setup

### Step 1: Add SIP trunk credentials in VAPI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open VAPI Integrations">
    Log in to the [VAPI Dashboard](https://dashboard.vapi.ai), click **Integrations** in the sidebar, then navigate to **SIP Trunk Credentials**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a new credential">
    Click **Add New Credential** and fill in the following fields:

    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Name | A friendly label, e.g. `Vobiz Trunk` |
    | Gateway URL | Your Vobiz SIP domain, e.g. `bfab10fb.sip.vobiz.ai` |
    | Username | Your Vobiz trunk username |
    | Password | Your Vobiz trunk password |
    | Outbound calling | Enabled |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. If VAPI accepts without an error, your systems are connected.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Your Vobiz SIP domain is unique to your trunk. Never use the generic `sip.vobiz.ai`—it will cause authentication failures.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Find your Vobiz credentials

If you don't have your credentials ready, retrieve them from the Vobiz Console:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Vobiz Console">
    Log in at [console.vobiz.ai](https://console.vobiz.ai) and go to **SIP Trunk → Outbound Trunks → Trunks**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your trunk">
    Click on your trunk and scroll down to the **Authentication & Linking** section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Copy credentials">
    Copy the **SIP Domain**, **Username**, and **Password**. These are the values you enter in Step 1 above.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 3: Import your Vobiz phone number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Phone Numbers">
    In the VAPI Dashboard, navigate to **Phone Numbers** and click **Add Number**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select BYO SIP Trunk">
    Choose **BYO SIP Trunk Number** from the options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Phone number | Your Vobiz number, e.g. `08071387149` |
    | SIP Trunk Credential | Select the credential you created in Step 1 |
    | Label | A friendly name, e.g. `Vobiz Main Line` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import">
    Click **Import SIP Trunk Number**. Your number now appears in VAPI with a real caller ID.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 4: Make an outbound call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a call">
    In the VAPI Dashboard, go to **Phone Calls → New Call** or click **Make a Call**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure outbound settings">
    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Phone number to call | Destination number in E.164 format, e.g. `+919148223344` |
    | Assistant | Select your VAPI assistant |
    | Phone number to call from | Your imported Vobiz number |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dial">
    Click **Make Outbound Call**. The destination phone rings and your AI assistant speaks when answered.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 5: Configure call transfer (optional)

To let the AI assistant transfer a call to a human agent:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Tools">
    In the VAPI Dashboard, go to the **Tools** section and click **Create Tool**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Transfer Call">
    Choose **Transfer Call** from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the destination">
    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Phone Number | Destination in E.164 format, e.g. `+919876543210` |
    | Transfer Mode | **Blind Transfer** |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. Blind transfer forwards the call immediately without the AI announcing the handoff.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Inbound calling setup

Allow callers to reach your VAPI AI assistant by calling your Vobiz number.

**Inbound call flow:**

```text
Caller Dials Vobiz Number → Vobiz SIP → VAPI SIP Trunk → VAPI AI Assistant → Conversation
```

### Inbound step 1: Create an inbound SIP trunk in VAPI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations">
    In the VAPI Dashboard, click **More → Integrations** in the sidebar and navigate to **SIP Trunk**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the trunk">
    Click **Create New SIP Trunk** and fill in:

    | Setting | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Name | e.g. `Vobiz Inbound Trunk` |
    | Gateway IP | `13.233.44.61` |
    | Port | `5060` |
    | Netmask | `32` |
    | Protocol | `UDP` |
    | Inbound Calls | Enabled |
    | Outbound Calls | Disabled |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save**. To support both inbound and outbound on one trunk, click **Add Another Gateway** and add your Vobiz SIP domain with credentials for outbound.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Inbound step 2: Get the VAPI trunk ID

After saving, retrieve the trunk ID using the VAPI API. You need this to configure Vobiz.

```bash
curl -X GET "https://api.vapi.ai/credential" \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer <YOUR_VAPI_API_KEY>"
```

Find the entry with gateway IP `13.233.44.61` and `outboundEnabled: false`. Copy the `id` field—this is your trunk ID.

```json
{
  "id": "46deea29-645d-44a3-bf42-7c6ec2d4b65c",
  "name": "Vobiz Inbound Trunk",
  "gateways": [
    {
      "ip": "13.233.44.61",
      "outboundEnabled": false
    }
  ]
}
```

### Inbound step 3: Create inbound trunk in Vobiz

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Inbound Trunks">
    Log in to [Vobiz Console](https://console.vobiz.ai) and go to **SIP Trunks → Inbound Trunk**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create the trunk">
    Click **Create New Trunk** and enter:

    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Name | e.g. `VAPI Inbound` |
    | Transport | `UDP` |
    | Primary URI | `<VAPI_TRUNK_ID>.sip.vapi.ai`, e.g. `46deea29-645d-44a3-bf42-7c6ec2d4b65c.sip.vapi.ai` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link your phone number">
    Attach your Vobiz phone number to this trunk, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Inbound step 4: Import number and assign assistant

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import the number in VAPI">
    In the VAPI Dashboard, go to **Phone Numbers → Create Phone Number → BYO SIP Trunk Number**. Enter your Vobiz number and select the inbound trunk created in step 1.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign the assistant">
    On the imported number, scroll to **Inbound Settings**, select your VAPI assistant, and click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test">
    Dial your Vobiz number from any phone. Your AI assistant answers and starts the conversation automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Call doesn't connect">
    Verify that the SIP domain, username, and password in VAPI exactly match those shown in **Vobiz Console → SIP Trunk → Authentication & Linking**. Use your specific domain like `bfab10fb.sip.vobiz.ai`, not the generic `sip.vobiz.ai`.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Assistant is silent after call connects">
    Check that your VAPI assistant has a **First Message** configured and that a voice provider is set up. Review call logs in VAPI Dashboard → Calls for error details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Phone number format error">
    Use E.164 format (`+919148223344` with leading `+`) or local format (`09148223344` without `+`). Verify the number is active in the Vobiz Console.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
