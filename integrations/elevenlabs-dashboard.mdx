This guide shows you how to connect your Vobiz phone number to ElevenLabs Conversational AI so your agent can make and receive real phone calls with ultra-realistic voices. The entire setup is done through the ElevenLabs dashboard—no code required. Expect to finish in about 15 minutes.

**Outbound call flow:**

```text
Your App → ElevenLabs Agent → Vobiz SIP → Phone Network → Person Answers → AI Agent Speaks
```

## Prerequisites

Before you start, make sure you have:

- A Vobiz account with an active SIP trunk — [sign up](https://console.vobiz.ai/auth/signup)
- An ElevenLabs account — [sign up](https://elevenlabs.io)
- An ElevenLabs agent already created — [create one](https://elevenlabs.io/app)
- A phone number purchased from Vobiz (for caller ID)

## Outbound calling setup

### Step 1: Import your phone number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Phone Numbers">
    Log in to the [ElevenLabs Dashboard](https://elevenlabs.io/app) and navigate to **Phone Numbers** in the sidebar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Import from SIP Trunk">
    Click **Import Number**, then select **Import from SIP Trunk**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Enter your phone number details

On the next screen, fill in:

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Label | A friendly name, e.g. `Vobiz Main Line` |
| Phone Number | Your Vobiz number with country code, e.g. `+911234567890` |

Click **Next** to continue to outbound configuration.

### Step 3: Configure outbound SIP trunk settings

Enter your Vobiz SIP trunk credentials. Find these in the [Vobiz Console](https://console.vobiz.ai) under **SIP Trunk → Outbound Trunks → Trunks → Your Trunk → Authentication & Linking**.

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| Transport Type | `TCP` |
| Address (SIP Trunk Address) | Your Vobiz SIP domain, e.g. `<your_domain>.sip.vobiz.ai` |
| SIP Trunk Username | Your Vobiz trunk username |
| SIP Trunk Password | Your Vobiz trunk password |

<Tip>
The **Address** field expects only the domain portion of your SIP URI. Do not include `sip:` or a port number.
</Tip>

Click **Save** or **Import** to complete the outbound setup.

### Step 4: Make your first call

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Phone Numbers">
    In the ElevenLabs Dashboard, navigate to **Phone Numbers** and select the number you just imported.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start a call">
    Click **Make Outbound Call**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the call">
    | Field | Value |
    |---|---|
    | Agent | Select your ElevenLabs conversational AI agent |
    | Destination number | Phone number to call with country code, e.g. `+919988776655` |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dial">
    Click **Call** or **Start Test Call**. The destination phone rings. When answered, your agent speaks the configured first message and the conversation begins.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To verify a call completed successfully, go to **Agents Platform → Your Agent → Call History** and review transcripts and recordings.

## Inbound calling setup

Allow callers to reach your ElevenLabs agent by dialing your Vobiz number.

### Step 1: Configure the inbound SIP URI in Vobiz

In your Vobiz SIP trunk configuration or application Answer URL, set the inbound destination to:

```text
sip.rtc.elevenlabs.io:5060
```

Use TCP transport. This tells Vobiz to forward all inbound calls on your number to ElevenLabs.

### Step 2: Import the number in ElevenLabs

Go to [ElevenLabs Phone Numbers](https://elevenlabs.io/app/agents/phone-numbers) and click **Import from SIP Trunk**. Follow the same steps as the outbound setup above to import your Vobiz number.

### Step 3: Attach the number to an agent

In the Phone Numbers list, locate the number you imported and click **Attach**. Select the agent you want to answer inbound calls.

To test, dial your Vobiz number from any phone. The selected ElevenLabs agent picks up and starts the conversation.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="SIP 408 Request Timeout">
    Verify your Vobiz SIP trunk address is correct (e.g. `abc123def.sip.vobiz.ai`) and that transport is set to `TCP`. Check that the trunk status shows as `active` in the [Vobiz Console](https://console.vobiz.ai).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="SIP authentication failed">
    Verify the username and password in **Vobiz Console → Trunks**. Credentials are case-sensitive. Re-enter them in the ElevenLabs dashboard exactly as shown.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Call connects but no audio">
    Ensure your ElevenLabs agent has a **First Message** configured and a valid voice selected. Test the voice in the ElevenLabs dashboard before placing a call. Check call logs in [ElevenLabs Dashboard](https://elevenlabs.io/app) for errors.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Call doesn't connect at all">
    Use E.164 format for the destination number (`+919988776655` with `+`) or local format (`09988776655` without `+`). Confirm your Vobiz trunk is active and check CDR logs in the Vobiz Console for call attempts.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
